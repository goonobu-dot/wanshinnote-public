Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

"Dog Heart Log" (the "App") respects your privacy to the fullest extent. This Policy explains how information is handled in the App.

1. Data Collection

**The App does not collect any personal information or usage data from its users.**

  • The App does not perform any network communication.
  • All information you enter (your pet's name, entries, notes, etc.) is stored only on your device.
  • No third party, including us, can access this information.

2. Where Data Is Stored

All information entered into the App is stored locally on your device. It is never sent to or stored on any external server or cloud service.

3. Disclosure to Third Parties

Because the App does not collect any information, there is no information to disclose to third parties.

4. Analytics and Advertising

The App does not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

5. Payments

Subscription and one-time purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Payment information is handled by Apple, and we never obtain or store any payment information. For details on how Apple handles your information, please refer to Apple's privacy policy.

6. Deleting Your Data

Information stored on your device can be erased by deleting individual entries within the App, or by deleting (uninstalling) the App itself.

7. Contact

For questions about this Policy, please contact the App's support channel listed on the App Store. We are unable to respond to individual questions about your pet's health — please consult your veterinarian.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Policy as necessary. The revised Policy takes effect when it is displayed within the App.

End of Policy
